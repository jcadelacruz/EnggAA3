Gym: Housing an olympic-sized pool, indoor basketball court, music room, dance room, clinic, and also a stage which serves as a back-up auditorium, the gymnasium is an all rounder building. The clinic is close to the sports area where injuries are more likely to happen, allowing for quick response time. The volleyball court has been fenced off, while the basketball court is located in an open area. The swimming pool is inside the gym complex to take advantage of its covered design. Keeping the pool indoors protects it from external elements allowing for year-round use without the burden of frequent cleaning or weather-related cancellations. In addition, the indoor setting ensures better maintenance of water temperature and cleanliness, supporting both physical education and extracurricular activities effectively. The clinic is located near the athletic and sports areas, recognizing that injuries are most likely to occur in physically active zones like the gym, courts, and field. By placing the clinic close to where accidents are more probable, we can ensure faster response times and easier access to first aid or emergency care. This enhances the overall safety infrastructure of the school and supports student wellness on a daily basis. Parking (Gym): Parking has been provided near the gym to make it easy for staff, coaches, and visitors to access the facility directly. This lot also acts as a convenient overflow option during peak hours or special events when the main parking lots are full. Its close proximity to the gym ensures that people can load equipment, bring in materials for events, or simply access the building more easily without disrupting other parts of the campus.
